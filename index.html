Intro

I'm an incoming PhD student in Computer Science at Cornell University. I recently received my B.E. in Computer Science and Technology from Tsinghua University. As an undergraduate, I have been fortunate to work with Prof. Han Zhang at Tsinghua, Prof. Minlan Yu at Harvard and Prof. Ziv Scully at Cornell.

My research interests lie at the intersection of computer systems, networks and theory. During my PhD, I aim to tackle challenges in networked systems through a theoretical lens, with an emphasis on reliability and efficiency. I also wish to expand my research to machine learning systems and its applications.
